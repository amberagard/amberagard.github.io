A little about me...

My name is Amber and I am from Georgia. I earned a Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Communications from Clemson University. After college, I spent a few months in Nashville before relocating to San Francisco for about a year and a half. While in San Francisco, I worked at GitHub, gaining a vast amount of knowledge about technology and web development. It was my time at GitHub that led me back to Nashville to attend the Nashville Software School to start pursuing a career in web development.
